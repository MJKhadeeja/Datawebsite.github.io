Diabetes prediction
project

Diabetes is a chronic disease that affects millions of people worldwide and poses significant health risks if not diagnosed and managed early. This project aims to predict the likelihood of diabetes in individuals by leveraging machine learning techniques and data analytics. Using a dataset containing various health parameters such as Glucose level, BMI, blood pressure, SkinThickness, Pregnancies and family history, we develop a predictive model to identify at-risk individuals. By applying classification algorithms and statistical analysis, the model provides insights into key factors contributing to diabetes risk. This study highlights the role of data-driven approaches in early detection and prevention, ultimately assisting healthcare professionals in improving patient outcomes and promoting proactive care.
